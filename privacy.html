Little Moves 隐私政策

Little Moves（以下简称「本应用」）重视您的隐私保护。本隐私政策旨在说明我们如何处理与您使用本应用相关的信息。

1. 信息收集说明

本应用当前版本不收集、不上传、不存储任何个人身份信息或用户数据。所有与您使用相关的数据，如运动记录、设置偏好、提醒配置、统计信息等，均仅存储在您的设备本地，不会传输至我们的服务器或任何第三方平台。

为实现核心功能，例如本地提醒、任务记录、统计展示等，本应用会在您的设备本地处理相关数据。该处理过程完全发生在您的设备内部，我们无法访问或获取这些信息。

若未来版本新增账号系统、云同步或其他需联网功能，我们将在更新本政策时，明确说明收集范围、目的与使用方式，并依法获取您的同意。

2. 信息的存储与安全

  • 本地存储：所有数据仅保存在您的设备本地。您可以随时通过卸载应用删除所有数据。
  • 数据安全：我们采用合理的技术措施保护应用内数据不被非法访问，但无法控制因设备丢失、损坏、系统故障或您自行操作导致的数据丢失或泄露。建议您自行做好重要数据备份。
  • 网络传输：当前版本无任何数据上传或联网传输行为。若后续新增网络功能，我们将采用加密传输等安全措施保障数据安全。

3. 系统权限与第三方服务

为实现基础功能，本应用可能需要请求以下系统权限。所有权限仅用于实现对应功能，不会用于其他目的：

  • 通知权限：用于发送运动或习惯提醒，权限由您在系统设置中自主控制。
  • 本应用不集成任何第三方统计、广告或追踪 SDK，不会向第三方共享任何信息。
  • 所有权限使用均遵循 Apple 的隐私规则。您可随时在系统设置中关闭权限授权。

4. 您的权利

  • 您可在系统设置中随时管理、关闭本应用的通知等权限。
  • 您可随时卸载本应用，所有本地数据将从您的设备中删除。
  • 如您对本隐私政策或数据处理有任何疑问，可通过本应用内提供的联系方式与我们联系。

5. 政策更新

我们可能根据产品迭代、法律法规更新或运营需求修订本隐私政策。重大变更将通过应用内公告、弹窗提示等合理方式告知您。修订后的政策自公布之日起生效，您继续使用本应用即视为接受更新后的条款。

6. 联系我们

如您对本隐私政策有任何疑问或建议，请通过以下方式联系我们：

road2wealthy@yeah.net

Little Moves Privacy Policy

Little Moves (hereinafter referred to as the “App”) values your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information related to your use of the App.

1. Information We Collect

The current version of the App does not collect, upload, or store any personally identifiable information or user data. All data related to your usage, such as activity records, settings preferences, reminder configurations, and statistical information, is stored locally on your device only and will not be transmitted to our servers or any third-party platforms.

To enable core functions, such as local reminders, task logging, and statistics display, the App processes relevant data locally on your device. This processing occurs entirely on your device, and we cannot access or obtain this information.

If future versions add account systems, cloud sync, or other internet-based features, we will clearly state the scope, purpose, and use of data collection in an updated version of this Policy and obtain your consent as required by applicable law.

2. Information Storage and Security

  • Local Storage: All data is stored only on your device. You may delete all data at any time by uninstalling the App.
  • Data Security: We implement reasonable technical measures to protect data within the App from unauthorized access. However, we cannot be responsible for data loss or disclosure caused by device loss, damage, system failure, or your own actions. You are advised to back up important data regularly.
  • Network Transmission: The current version does not upload or transmit any data over the network. If network features are added in the future, we will adopt security measures such as encrypted transmission to protect data security.

3. System Permissions and Third-Party Services

To enable basic functions, the App may request the following system permissions. All permissions are used solely for their intended purposes and not for any other uses:

  • Notification Permission: Used to send activity or habit reminders. You may control this permission in your system settings.
  • The App does not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking SDKs and will not share any information with third parties.
  • All permission usage complies with Apple’s privacy rules. You may revoke permissions at any time in your system settings.

4. Your Rights

  • You may manage or disable notification and other permissions for the App at any time in your device settings.
  • You may uninstall the App at any time, and all local data will be removed from your device.
  • If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or data processing, you may contact us via the contact information provided in the App.

5. Policy Updates

We may revise this Privacy Policy from time to time in response to product updates, legal requirements, or operational needs. Material changes will be notified to you via in-app announcements, pop-up prompts, or other reasonable means. The revised Policy will take effect upon publication, and your continued use of the App constitutes acceptance of the updated terms.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions or suggestions regarding this Privacy Policy, please contact us at:

road2wealthy@yeah.net
