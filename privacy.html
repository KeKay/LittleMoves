适用范围 / Scope

本隐私政策仅适用于 iOS 应用「随手动动 / Little Moves」。

This Privacy Policy applies only to the iOS app “Little Moves”.

English Version

Introduction

Little Moves is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how we handle your data.

Core Privacy Principles

  • Local-only storage: Your activity records and app settings are stored only on your device.
  • Minimal data handling: We do not collect personal information from you.
  • No cloud upload: Your data is not uploaded to our servers.
  • User control: You can manage or delete your local data at any time.

Local Data Storage

All data in Little Moves is stored only on your local device, including:

  • Workout task settings
  • Reminder preferences
  • Completed activity records
  • App configuration and usage preferences

This data is protected by iOS system security mechanisms and safeguarded by your device passcode and biometric protections provided by Apple.

Information We Do Not Collect

  • Personal identity information (such as your legal name or ID number)
  • Contact information (such as phone number or email address), unless you contact us directly
  • Precise location data
  • Advertising identifiers
  • Contacts, photos, or social relationship data
  • Health data from Apple Health, unless explicitly stated in a future version
  • Financial or payment account information
  • Biometric data

How Your Data Is Used

Your locally stored data is used only for the following purposes within the app:

  • Generating random movement or exercise reminders
  • Displaying your task completion history
  • Saving your preferences and custom activity settings
  • Improving your in-app experience on your device

Third-Party Services

Little Moves is designed to be lightweight and privacy-conscious:

  • No third-party advertising SDKs
  • No third-party analytics or tracking services
  • No social media integration
  • No cloud sync service for user data
  • No external API required to process your local activity records

Children’s Privacy

Little Moves is not designed to knowingly collect personal information from children.

Privacy Policy Updates

If we make significant changes to this Privacy Policy:

  • We may update this page and revise the “Last updated” date
  • Important changes may also be noted in App Store release notes
  • Your continued use of the app after updates indicates acceptance of the revised policy

中文版

引言

随手动动 致力于保护您的隐私。本政策说明我们如何处理与应用相关的数据。

核心隐私原则

  • 仅本地存储：您的运动记录和应用设置仅保存在您的设备上
  • 最小化数据处理：我们不主动收集您的个人信息
  • 不上传云端：您的数据不会上传到我们的服务器
  • 用户可控：您可以随时管理或删除本地数据

本地数据存储

随手动动 的所有数据仅存储在您的本地设备上，包括：

  • 动作任务设置
  • 提醒偏好设置
  • 已完成的运动记录
  • 应用配置与使用偏好

这些数据依赖 iOS 系统提供的安全机制进行保护，并受到设备密码及 Apple 提供的生物识别保护措施的保障。

我们不收集的信息

  • 个人身份信息（如真实姓名、身份证号等）
  • 联系方式（如手机号码、邮箱地址等，除非您主动联系我们）
  • 精确位置信息
  • 广告标识符
  • 通讯录、照片或社交关系数据
  • Apple Health 健康数据，除非未来版本明确说明接入
  • 金融账户或支付账户信息
  • 任何形式的生物识别数据

数据用途

您保存在本地设备中的数据，仅用于应用内以下功能：

  • 生成随机运动或动作提醒
  • 展示您的任务完成记录
  • 保存您的偏好和自定义动作设置
  • 在您的设备上优化应用使用体验

第三方服务

随手动动 采用轻量、注重隐私的设计：

  • 不接入第三方广告 SDK
  • 不接入第三方分析或追踪服务
  • 不连接社交媒体平台
  • 不提供用户数据云同步服务
  • 不依赖外部 API 处理您的本地运动记录

儿童隐私

随手动动 不会主动面向儿童收集个人信息。

隐私政策更新

如果我们对本隐私政策进行重要更新：

  • 我们可能会更新本页面，并修改“最后更新”日期
  • 重要改动也可能会在 App Store 更新说明中提示
  • 在政策更新后继续使用本应用，即视为您接受修订后的政策
